Proceedings of the Third International Workshop on Machine Learning in Systems Biology: Revised Selected Papers

Sašo Džeroski, Pierre Geurts, Juho Rousu; JMLR W&CP 8:1-2, 2010.

Abstract

MLSB09, the Third International Workshop on Machine Learning in Systems Biology was held in Ljubljana, Slovenia on September 5-6 2009 at the Jožef Stefan Institute. This volume contains revised selected papers presented at the workshop. The technical program of the workshop consisted of 6 invited lectures, 12 oral presentations and 22 poster presentations. All the lectures were recorded and are available for viewing via the videolectures.net portal. More information on the workshop can be found at mlsb09.ijs.si
